GREEN · MEASURED

The Nyquist limit fs/2 and the alias frequency |f − fs·round(f/fs)| are computed exactly, live. The banana crawling backward on screen is the real, arithmetic consequence — not a drawing trick.

AMBER · FIGURE

The orbiting banana is a chosen stand-in for continuous change. Any smoothly moving thing would alias the same way; the fruit is just so you can see which way it turns.

RED · THE WALL
